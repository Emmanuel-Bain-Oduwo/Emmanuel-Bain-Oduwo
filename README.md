## 👨‍💻 About Me

I work at the intersection of **pharmacy, artificial intelligence, and healthcare** — building clinical AI systems, medication safety infrastructure, and healthcare datasets that help healthcare professionals make safer, more informed decisions.

My flagship project, **[Kemirix](https://kemirix.com)**, is a clinical medication safety platform that ensures the rational and safe use of medications. It checks every prescription against the patient's full clinical context — conditions, vital signs, allergies, organ dysfunction, lab results, and genetics — helping doctors, pharmacists, and nurses prescribe and administer drugs safely while reducing prescription errors.

Alongside clinical AI, I build AI-powered educational technology platforms and open-source healthcare datasets that support research and learning in Africa and beyond.

<details open>
<summary><b>🏥 Kemirix Health Technologies</b> &nbsp;|&nbsp; <code>Clinical AI Platform</code> &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🚧 Active Development</summary>

<br/>

**Problem**
Medication errors remain a significant and preventable source of patient harm across healthcare systems globally — particularly in resource-limited settings where clinical decision support tools are scarce.

**Solution**
A clinical medication safety platform ensuring the rational and safe use of medications. Helps doctors, pharmacists, and nurses prescribe and administer drugs safely by checking every prescription against the patient's full clinical context: current condition, vital signs, allergies, organ dysfunction, laboratory results, and genetics. Maintains patient health records for safer prescriptions over time.

**Impact**
Reduces and prevents prescription errors at the point of care. Designed for the African healthcare context with a roadmap toward PPB Kenya Class II SaMD registration.

**Stack**
`FastAPI` `PostgreSQL` `MedGemma 27B` `Vertex AI` `Next.js` `Redis` `Alembic` `JWT`

**Links**
🌐 [kemirix.com](https://kemirix.com) &nbsp;|&nbsp; 📊 [Dataset on HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Oduwo/kemirix_category1_ddi)

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>💊 Kemirix DDI Database — Category 1</b> &nbsp;|&nbsp; <code>Drug Interaction Dataset</code> &nbsp;|&nbsp; ✅ Published</summary>

<br/>

**Problem**
Training clinical AI systems on drug-drug interactions requires structured, high-quality datasets that are difficult to compile from fragmented clinical sources.

**Solution**
Curated clinical drug interaction database — 26 anaesthetic drugs, 334 DDI pairs, 7 JSONL task types per scenario, across 6 safety classes: `ABSOLUTE_CONTRAINDICATION` → `INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE`. Includes East African pharmacogenomic variants.

**Stack**
`Python` `PostgreSQL` `JSONL` `HuggingFace`

**Links**
🤗 [Oduwo/kemirix_category1_ddi](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Oduwo/kemirix_category1_ddi)

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🧠 Clinical LLM Fine-Tuning Infrastructure</b> &nbsp;|&nbsp; <code>MLOps / AI Infrastructure</code> &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🚧 Active</summary>

<br/>

**Problem**
Fine-tuning large language models on clinical data requires specialized infrastructure and expertise that most healthcare AI teams lack.

**Solution**
Production-ready pipeline for healthcare LLM training using Google Cloud TPU v5litepod-16, enabling efficient fine-tuning of MedGemma 27B with SPMD/FSDPv2 and PEFT techniques including LoRA. Full training target: 1.44M JSONL lines across 14 KEML therapeutic categories.

**Stack**
`PyTorch/XLA` `JAX` `SPMD` `FSDPv2` `PEFT` `LoRA` `GCP TPU v5e` `GCS`

</details>
